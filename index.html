What is MyScribe

Every professional has a record of that meeting. Now you do too.

MyScribe gives you your own record of every professional encounter. Plain language. On your phone. Before you reach the door.

Free forever. MyScribe does not charge patients or citizens. It never will. The app is free to download and free to use. You are not the product.

How it works

One sentence. Then everything else is automatic.

1

Open the app

No login. No setup. MyScribe opens straight to the record screen.

2

Ask if you can record

"Do you mind if I record this, just for my own records?" That is the only thing you need to say or do.

3

Press record. Have your appointment.

One button. The AVT runs quietly. Be present in the conversation.

4

Press stop. Your After Note arrives.

Everything you need to understand and act on, in plain language, on your phone before you reach the door. MyScribe can read it to you aloud, in any language, in the waiting room, on the bus, or when you get home. Everything happens on your phone. No signal needed.

What an After Note looks like

A document designed entirely for you.

The After Note contains nothing the professional did not say. It selects, clarifies, and structures. What was agreed. What to do next. What to watch for. Who to contact if things change. The bar is use. Everything else gets left behind.

Questions

The ones people ask.

"Who pays for this if it's free to me?"
The deployer pays, never the citizen. Whoever builds the After Note methodology into their product or platform carries the cost. An AVT supplier, a GP system, a housing provider, a legal tech company. The After Note is free to receive because the cost sits entirely with the people who chose to offer it.
"Can I use it for any appointment?"
Any professional encounter where a record matters. GP, social worker, mental health worker, housing officer, solicitor, probation officer, benefits assessor. Any conversation where the other person leaves with notes and you leave with memory.
"What if the professional says no?"
That is their right. MyScribe does not record without consent from both people in the room. If they decline, you thank them and put your phone away. The consent sentence is a genuine question, not a formality.
"Is this more digital exclusion?"
The NHS App needs a login, identity verification, and confidence navigating a multi-layered interface. MyScribe needs one spoken sentence. Voice is the oldest and most universal human technology. It was designed from the start to reach the people that interface-first tools leave behind.
"What does it do with my data?"
Your After Note lives on your device. There is no archive. No account means no data held centrally. When MyScribe reads your After Note to you, that happens on your phone too. No third party server. No internet connection required. The note is yours. Even the voice that reads it to you never leaves your phone.
